SO WHAT IS JSLOU?

JSLou is a 100% free to attend community gathering where people of any experience level, and all types of JavaScript can feel right at home. JSLou is the first JavaScript focussed community group in Louisville Kentucky and was Established September 2013. JSLou was founded by Alex Bezuska.
